Terms & Conditions

By handing over any shipment to Mayl Logistics, the shipper agrees to the following Terms & Conditions.

1. Consignment Acceptance

Mayl Logistics only accepts flyer / document-based lightweight shipments.

  • Accepted Limit: Maximum weight: Up to 500 grams to (5 kg) only
  • Not Accepted: Box parcels, Heavy weight items, Bulk cargo, Large shipments

2. Shipper Responsibility

The shipper confirms that:

  • All shipment details are correct and complete
  • Receiver information (name, address, phone) is accurate
  • Parcel is properly packed for flyer handling
  • Fragile items must be clearly declared

Important: Undeclared fragile items are sent at sender’s own risk.

3. Rates & Charges

Mayl Logistics flyer delivery rates are as follows:

  • Local City Flyer Delivery: Rs. 140 – Rs. 250 (depending on weight)
  • Urgent / Same Day Flyer Service: Additional charges apply
  • COD Service Charges: As per agreed business contract

Rates may vary based on route, urgency, and service type.

4. Right of Inspection

Mayl Logistics reserves the right to inspect or open any shipment for security, legal compliance, or operational purposes.

5. Liability Policy

In case of loss or damage, Mayl Logistics liability is limited to:

  • Maximum compensation: Cost Price on per shipment
  • Or actual declared value (whichever is lower, if proof is provided)

6. Lost / Stolen Shipment Policy (Claim Condition)

In case of confirmed loss or theft during transit:

  • Claim will be processed only after full investigation
  • Claim amount will be based on cost price only (no profit included)
  • Selling price, profit margin, or business loss will NOT be covered
  • Valid purchase invoice or cost proof is required

7. Claim Duration & Procedure

  • Claim must be reported within 24–48 hours of incident
  • Investigation period may take up to 10 weeks
  • Claims submitted after 10 weeks will not be accepted
  • Claims will be processed only after clearance of all dues

8. COD Policy

  • COD amount will be transferred as per agreed schedule
  • Sender is responsible for correct COD declaration
  • COD disputes must be reported immediately
  • If COD parcel is returned, return charges will apply

9. Delivery Delays

Delays may occur due to weather conditions, road blockage or traffic issues, operational or technical issues, incorrect address or contact number, or force majeure situations. Mayl Logistics is not responsible for indirect or business losses due to delay.

10. Delivery Attempts

Up to 5 delivery attempts will be made. If delivery fails, the shipment may be returned to sender and return charges will apply.

11. Prohibited Items

Mayl Logistics does NOT accept cash or currency, jewelry, gold, silver, weapons or explosives, drugs or illegal substances, hazardous or flammable materials, live animals, perishable goods, or any illegal item under Pakistani law.

12. Privacy Policy

All customer data is kept confidential and used only for delivery and operational purposes.

13. Acceptance of Terms

By using Mayl Logistics services, the shipper confirms that they have read, understood, and agreed to all Terms & Conditions mentioned above.
